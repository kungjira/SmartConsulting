About Smart Consulting Co., Ltd.

Smart Consulting Co., Ltd. is established in 1995 by built-up the team of top level administrators with over 30 years experienced in the fields of Accounting (Both Financial and Managerial Accounting) Finance and Administration in various types of business such as Industry, Services Hotel, Import-Export, Automobile Business, Banking and Financial Institutions.

Smart Consulting Co., Ltd. provides full professional services and consulting to cope customer's business management information system.

Smart Consulting Co., Ltd. is one of Sage 300 ERP (Sage ERP Accpac) Solution Provider - Platinum Level Status of Sage Singapore Pte. Ltd. the leader of Accounting Solutions and Business Solutions which is able to serve any requirement of various businesses for both Financial and Managerial Accounting by design.
