Customized Programing & Report Services

Auto Calculate Online Submission For Withholding TAX

Auto Calculate Online Submission For Withholding TAX help estimate tax liabilities based on income and filing status, improving accuracy, aiding financial planning, reducing errors, and saving time.

Auto Reverse VAT

The reverse charge mechanism lets buyers account for VAT on cross-border transactions without immediate payment, enhancing financial accuracy and cash flow while preventing tax evasion.

Auto Running Voucher Number

A feature that automates unique identifier generation for financial transactions boosts efficiency, minimizes errors, enables customized tracking, and enhances audit readiness and account reconciliation.

Electronic Fund Transfer for Bank Services (EFT)

Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) allows fast and secure electronic transactions for direct deposits and bill payments. It improves convenience and financial management for both individuals and businesses.
