Sage Cover

"Your Protection in Mind"

Sage Cover is our umbrella brand for comprehensive software service and assurance.

What is Sage Cover?

Sage Cover is our umbrella brand for comprehensive software service and assurance.

With Sage Cover, you do not need to worry about keeping up to date with the latest version because it will be made available to you automatically. Each new version includes features that help your team maximize their productivity and performance. And best of all, it cost significantly less on an annual basis than sporadically purchasing new software licenses.

Benefits of Sage Cover

Maximize productivity

Your software will be insured and protected atall times to ensure its efficiency. Thus increase the productivity of your operation.

Protect your investment

Because you are always running the most up-todate software, you do not need to worry thatyour business-critical software may become obsolete.

Save Time

You need not manually watch out for new software versions. We, Sage and your Business Partner, will notify you on software updates and will make new versions readily available to you as soon as they are released.

Save Money

Just a small percentage of your module SRP compared to buying new licenses of new version at full price. Why pay more for new version when you can have it with Sage Cover for less?

Protect Against Price Increase

When SRP prices increase, you are already covered with Sage Cover. One year and discounted multi year subscription terms allow you to choose the degree of price protection that is right for your business and help simplify your budgeting.

Special benefits for

Sage Cover members

All New version Update and Service Packs

You will be entitled to upgrade your Sage 300 ERP system to the latest version, along with access to download service packs and hotfixes. These updates are designed to enhance the performance and efficiency of your current Sage 300 ERP system.

Online Account Access

You will be provided with a username and password to access the Online Technical Support platform, as well as the e-Learning system for comprehensive training on the usage of Sage 300 ERP.

Assurance of Application Compatibility to Operating System

Upgrading the Sage 300 ERP system to the latest versions enhances compatibility and performance with new operating systems and software, such as Windows and Microsoft SQL Server.

Exclusive Benefits for Purchasing Additional Sage 300 ERP Products

You have the option to purchase additional modules tailored to your business requirements or expand the number of LanPak users at any time during your Sage Cover membership. Additionally, you may upgrade to a higher edition to enhance operational efficiency and support the continued growth of your organization.
