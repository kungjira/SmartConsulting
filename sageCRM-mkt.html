Create measurable marketing campaigns with Sage CRM and Mailchimp

Sage CRM integrates with Mailchimp, allowing you to effortlessly add your Sage CRM contacts to your Mailchimp audience. You can create and manage targeted email marketing campaigns directly within Sage CRM, using your customer data. Plus, you can view key performance metrics like opens, clicks, and bounces right in Sage CRM. Additionally, you can segment your contacts in Sage CRM and use these segments to craft targeted Mailchimp campaigns.
