Orchid Systems

Sage 300 Add-Ons

They enhance productivity, improve collaboration, support better decision making, and push out the boundaries of Sage 300 beyond what you thought was possible.

Collaboration

Create, locate, update and share information in intuitive, powerful ways that can be easily integrated into your everyday use of Sage 300. Use Notes & Document Management Link on their own, or combined with other Orchid products. Use TeamWork for mobile, chat-based collaboration and management.

Notes

The easy way to attach context-sensitive notes to Sage 300 screens

Document Management Link

Easy access to essential documents directly from your Sage 300 screens

TeamWork for Sage 300

Mobile, chat-based collaboration and management for Sage 300

Tailoring, Automation & Integration

Codeless tailoring of Sage 300, bespoke customization using Python, automation of critical processes and integration with external applications. This category features Orchid Extender, Process Scheduler, Remote Action Service and Workflow Anywhere.

Extender

A cost-effective way to configure, customize and extend Sage 300

Process Scheduler

Schedule your critical business processes to run automatically and unattended

Remote Action Service

Progress an Extender Workflow without needing to be logged in to Sage 300

Workflow Anywhere

An Extender Workflow Console for your mobile device

Streamlining Financial Processes

Improve productivity and increase accuracy while taking the drudgery out of processing complex but routine transactions. This category features EFT Processing, Inter-Entity Transactions and Inter-Entity Trade.

EFT Processing (for Sage 300)

Streamline Sage 300 payments & receipts using Electronic Funds Transfer

Inter-Entity Transactions

Keep Sage 300 entities in balance at all times

Inter-Entity Trade

Automates Sage 300 transaction flows when related entities trade with each other

Budgeting, Reporting & Business Intelligence

Manage custom data, take control of your key reports, analyse business performance, and integrate granular budgeting & forecasting into Sage 300. This category features Info-Explorer, Optional Tables, Data Views and Report Runner.

Info-Explorer

A powerful, affordable BI tool supporting integrated analysis & budgeting

Optional Tables

Create custom, multi-dimensional data structures within your Sage 300 database

Data Views

Simplify reporting of Sage 300 Optional Fields

Report Runner

Streamline the setup, execution, and distribution of Crystal or Sage 300 Financial Reports

Operations & Inventory Management

Tightly integrate your operational processes into Sage 300, improving both productivity and customer service. This category features Remote Material Authorizations (RMA), Bin Tracking and Freight SmartLink.

Return Material Authorizations

Accurately track returns & repairs from within Sage 300

Bin Tracking

Track inventory items accurately across multiple bins within Sage 300

Freight SmartLink

Streamline integration between Sage 300 and your freight carriers
